**Autophag-X** is an unprecedented AI robotics framework designed for extreme exploration missions. It implements **Autophagic Robotics**, where robots are built from energy-dense structural polymers and can strategically consume their own parts for power when external energy is unavailable.

## 🚀 The Vision
In deep space, subterranean voids, or oceanic trenches, energy is the ultimate constraint. Autophag-X shifts the paradigm from "carrying batteries" to "being the battery." 

When energy reserves reach critical levels, the **Autophagic Decision Engine (ADE)** calculates which physical component provides the highest energy gain with the lowest impact on mission mobility, allowing the robot to survive and continue its mission by literally eating itself.

## 🛠 Key Features
- **Autophagic Decision Engine (ADE):** RL-based model for strategic self-consumption.
- **Dynamic Kinematic Re-learning:** Real-time gait adjustment as mass and geometry change.
- **Structural Energy Harvesting:** Technical specifications for PEEK-Nitrated structural fuels.
- **Sacrificial Architecture:** Modular design for staged degradation.

## 📁 Project Structure
- `autophagx/`: Core AI and Simulation logic.
- `tests/`: Validation scripts for re-learning algorithms.
- `docs/`: Technical specifications and mathematical models.
- `main.py`: Entry point for the autophagic mission simulation.
